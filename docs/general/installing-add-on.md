# Installing an Add-on

You can view [the list of available add-ons here](/../addons).

::: tip Important
The **Loggedin** plugin must be installed and active for the add-on to work.
:::

## Installing the Add-on

To install an add-on, you must first purchase a license. After your purchase is complete, you will receive an email containing a license key and a link to download the add-on's `.zip` file.

* Download the add-on .zip file from the link provided in your email.
* Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
* Go to **Plugins > Add New**.
* Click the **Upload Plugin** button.
* Select the add-on `.zip` file you just downloaded and click **Install Now**.
* Once the installation is complete, click **Activate Plugin**.

## Activating the Add-on License

Activating your license is necessary to receive updates, which include new features, improvements, and bug fixes.

* From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Loggedin plugin's admin user interface.
* Go to the **Addons** page.
* If the add-on is correctly installed and activated, you will have an input field for you to enter your license key.
* Enter the license key you received in your email and click the **Activate** button.

That's it! Your add-on is now installed, activated, and ready to receive updates 🎉

If you are facing any issues with installation or activation, feel free to [contact our support](https://duckdev.com/contact/).
